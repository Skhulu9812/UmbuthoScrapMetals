About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and commitment to sustainability

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Umbutho Scrap Metals began as a small family business with a vision to create a sustainable future through responsible metal recycling. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted industry leader, serving both individual customers and commercial clients across the region.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, reliable, and environmentally responsible metal recycling services that benefit our customers, community, and planet.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in sustainable metal recycling, driving innovation and setting new standards for environmental stewardship in our industry.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing environmental impact through responsible recycling practices that conserve natural resources.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and fairness in all our dealings with customers and partners.

Community

We actively support and invest in our local community, creating jobs and promoting environmental awareness.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our operations, from customer service to processing efficiency.

Our Services

Comprehensive metal recycling solutions tailored to your needs

Scrap Metal Collection

We offer convenient pickup services for both residential and commercial clients, handling all types of ferrous and non-ferrous metals.

Metal Recycling

Our advanced processing facilities ensure that all collected metals are properly sorted, cleaned, and prepared for recycling.

Precise Weighing

We use certified, state-of-the-art scales to ensure accurate weighing and fair pricing for all your scrap materials.

Processing & Sorting

Our skilled team efficiently processes and sorts different metal types to maximize recycling efficiency and value.

Instant Payment

We provide immediate payment based on current market rates for all accepted materials, with no hidden fees.

On-site Demolition

For larger projects, we offer professional on-site demolition and metal recovery services to simplify your recycling process.
